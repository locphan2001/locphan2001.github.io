I am happy to give some advice and help students "at no money cost", look for master's scholarships in Europe. For students who come from Mathematics at <a href='https://hcmue.edu.vn/en/'>Ho Chi Minh University of Education</a>, if you are 
- currently in year 3 or year 4,
- rank over 25% of the class,
- want to look for any opportunity to study for a master's degree in Europe,
  
don't hesitate to send me an email.

In Europe, publications were given very little weight in the evaluation for the master level, so do not lack confidence due to the absence of those. I do not guarantee getting the scholarship, but I will try my best to put you in the best preparations: scholarships currently (or future) open for applications, resume, cover letter, etc. 

Preparing for study abroad is a long journey, so think carefully and start as soon as possible, and you also should check [Master scholarships for higher education in different regions](scholarship/) to know more about how scholarships work. 

Do not wait until you finish your current program to start thinking about it.
